Surface 4 · the way back in

The planner header entry point

A persistent control in the Energy Planner page header (alongside the info tooltip and Import / Export) that links to /energy-planner/wellness. It's the durable way back after opting out — so it must be visible regardless of on/off state. Options differ in how loud it is and whether it signals status.

H1 · Labeled button

A plain “Wellness” button matching the existing header actions. Clear and discoverable; adds one item to the row.
